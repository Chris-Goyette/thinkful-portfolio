Hi. I'm Chris Goyette!

I am a software developer living in the Bay Area.

In my last job I was working in fintech as a Fraud Analyst for a peer-to-peer lending company.

After getting laid off, I realized that I wanted more in life and that I needed to be more valuable to society. That's when I decided that I wanted to level up my skills and become a software developer.

About Me

Getting laid off during the novel Covid-19 pandemic has been a real wake up call to me. Working in Operations made me quickly realize just how vulnerable my job was.

My professional background is in credit underwriting and fraud analysis. As much as I enjoyed my profession, I realized that I needed to learn more technical skills if I want to stay competitive in the workforce.

I have always been fascinated with technology and all the things you can create with it. The reason why I chose to pursue web development and software engineering is because I want to be able to create and build those things myself. I feel that I have a lot to contribute to the world and I want to do my part.
